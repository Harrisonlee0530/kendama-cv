    Features per frame

    • 33 MediaPipe pose landmarks (x, y, visibility)
    • Key joint angles (wrists, elbows, shoulders)
    • Ken orientation angle (degrees from vertical)
    • Tama position relative to ken tip/base

    Tricks

    • Spike — tama lands on ken spike
    • Big Cup — tama lands in large cup
    • Lighthouse — ken balances on tama
    • Around Japan — tama orbits ken full rotation
    • Airplane — tama swings to spike via hole
    • Bird — balance trick with string taut
